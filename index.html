FAQs

Count on our seasoned IT professionals
to provide insightful answers

  • 01 How experienced is your team?

    We have 5+ years of combined experience and have partnered with organisations such as the National Healthcare Group (NHG), Synapse, Tan Tock Seng Hospital (TTSH), Nanyang Technological University's Lee Kong Chian School of Medicine (LKCMedicine) and the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) to deliver high-impact communication-skills training in both academic and workplace settings.

  • 02 What sets EmpathIQ apart from others?

    We combine advanced conversational AI with locally relevant scenarios to give learners realistic practice and instant, data-driven feedback that one-size-fits-all platforms can't match.

  • 03 How can I be sure the solution fits my organisation's needs?

    Book a free consultation with us. We'll clarify your objectives and, if there's a fit, run a low-risk pilot with a small user group so you can assess the results before committing to a full rollout.

  • 04 How do you ensure data security and privacy?

    We meet hospital-grade security standards: end-to-end encryption, role-based access controls, regular penetration tests and full compliance with PDPA/HIPAA guidelines. Only authorised users can access your data.

  • 05 Can you customize your services to meet our need?

    Absolutely! We tailor scenarios, analytics and system integrations to match your goals, workflows and technical constraints. Let's discuss how we can build the right fit for your team.
